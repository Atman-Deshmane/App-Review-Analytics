## Weekly App Review Pulse: com.vlv.aravali Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 21 Nov 2025 to 30 Nov 2025

### Executive Summary

The review landscape this period is dominated by a critical financial crisis, with the "Unauthorized Auto-Pay & Refunds" theme accounting for nearly 90% (593 of 665) of the total negative impact score. Users are reporting immediate, unexpected, and high-value charges (up to $\text{\textcurrency} 499$) applied without consent following initial payments, leading to severe trust degradation and urgent refund demands. Immediate cross-functional intervention is required to audit the subscription payment gateway and protect platform credibility.

### 3. Recommended Actions

1.  **Subscription Trust Audit:** Immediately establish an engineering and finance task force to perform a critical audit of all auto-renewal/trial-to-paid subscription flow logic, specifically investigating the reported unauthorized jump to the $\text{\textcurrency} 499$ plan and interactions with third-party payment gateways (e.g., PhonePe). Prioritize resolving all active refund demands stemming from this issue.
2.  **App Stability and UX Hotfix:** Prioritize a swift hotfix to address critical functional bugs: the UI regression preventing users from viewing episode titles and the highly severe bug that logs users out and *deletes* downloaded files when offline. These failures compromise the core value proposition of a paid audio service.
3.  **Content Pipeline Review:** Conduct an immediate check on the content release schedule for currently trending stories to address the complaint of "no updates for over a month." Leverage the positive feedback (Impact Score 27) by accelerating efforts to acquire or produce requested content (more business category books and latest titles).
