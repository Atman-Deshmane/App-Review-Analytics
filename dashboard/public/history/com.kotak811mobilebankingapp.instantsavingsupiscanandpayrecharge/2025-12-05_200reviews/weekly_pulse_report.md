## Weekly App Review Pulse: com.kotak811mobilebankingapp.instantsavingsupiscanandpayrecharge Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 05 Oct 2025 to 01 Dec 2025

### Executive Summary

The primary focus of user conversation remains highly concentrated on **Login Issues & App Stability** (4488 Impact Score), though the most visible review within this theme offers strong praise for our streamlined account opening process compared to competitors. However, significant friction persists in two critical areas: Feature Parity (users are forced to switch between the 811 and older apps) and a systemic failure in **Poor Customer Support**, particularly when dealing with account blocks and unauthorized financial deductions.

### 3. Recommended Actions

1.  **Prioritize 811 Feature Parity:** Immediately fast-track the integration of key missing features (e.g., Credit Score check and other self-service utilities) into the 811 app to eliminate the current workflow requiring users to switch between applications.
2.  **Restructure Blocked Account Resolution:** Audit and restructure the blocked account escalation protocol. Implement a 24/7 dedicated digital channel (in-app chat/email) for resolving critical issues like account blocks, ensuring users are not required to recharge mobile talktime or visit a branch for initial support.
3.  **Investigate Unauthorized Deductions:** Launch an urgent investigation into the "Unexpected Charges & Blocks" theme (617 Impact Score), specifically resolving all outstanding cases related to unauthorized debits for insurance policies or unfulfilled welcome kit fees, ensuring immediate refunds where applicable.
