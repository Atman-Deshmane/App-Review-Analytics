## Weekly App Review Pulse: com.swishapp Leadership Briefing

**Reporting Period:** 07 Nov 2025 to 01 Dec 2025

### Executive Summary

Review volume remains low (24 total reviews), but feedback is highly concentrated on a single critical operational failure: **Food Taste and Quality**. The severe impact score (38) and associated customer quotes suggest potential health risks (stomach sickness) tied to product consumption, demanding immediate quality control intervention. This critical safety failure is compounded by perceptions of high pricing and dysfunctional discount mechanisms.

### 3. Recommended Actions

1.  **URGENT Quality and Safety Audit:** Immediately launch a full-scale review of food preparation processes, storage, and ingredient sourcing across all operating kitchens. Prioritize investigation into the connection between rapid delivery times and reported customer sickness incidents.
2.  **Implement Tier 1 Support Triage:** Deploy a basic, real-time support mechanism (e.g., live chat bot or dedicated payment-support email alias) specifically to handle critical issues like failed payments and money deductions, drastically reducing resolution time for high-friction scenarios.
3.  **Refactor Discount Transparency:** Review and potentially decommission misleading promotional features like the "spinner." Replace these with clear, high-value, and reliable discount codes or offers to rebuild customer trust in pricing and promotional fairness.
