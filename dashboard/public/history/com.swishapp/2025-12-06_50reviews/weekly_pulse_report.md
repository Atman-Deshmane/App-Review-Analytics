## Weekly App Review Pulse: com.swishapp Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 23 Nov 2025 to 05 Dec 2025

### Executive Summary

The current reporting period highlights strong positive affirmation of Swish's core value proposition, with **Delivery Speed & Packaging** (Impact 7) and **Food Quality & Taste** (Impact 6) dominating the positive sentiment, often benchmarked favorably against key competitors. Leadership should capitalize on the established operational excellence while immediately addressing the emerging friction point noted by users regarding inventory management and product availability ("out of stock" issues).

### 3. Recommended Actions

1.  **Immediate Inventory Audit and Fix:** Prioritize the investigation into product availability issues, specifically addressing why users are reporting items as universally "out of stock." Implement better real-time inventory synchronization to prevent ordering friction.
2.  **Marketing Campaign Focus:** Leverage the direct competitor comparisons (Zomato, Swiggy, Bistro) found in the positive reviews. Focus Q1 marketing efforts on promoting "Guaranteed Freshness" and "Superior Packaging" as Swish's undeniable market advantage.
3.  **Operational Excellence Deep Dive:** Conduct a Root Cause Analysis (RCA) on the processes driving high impact in Delivery Speed and Packaging to ensure scalability and operational redundancy, minimizing risk to our primary differentiators.
