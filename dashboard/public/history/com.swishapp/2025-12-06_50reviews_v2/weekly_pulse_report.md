## Weekly App Review Pulse: com.swishapp Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 23 Nov 2025 to 05 Dec 2025

### Executive Summary

The reporting period, which garnered 36 total reviews, saw strong validation of our core product, with "Food Quality & Taste" generating the highest impact (Score 28) and driving overwhelmingly positive sentiment. However, operational issues regarding inventory visibility surfaced prominently, captured under the high-impact "Other" category (Score 13), suggesting widespread friction points related to item availability and stocking. Maintaining momentum requires immediate attention to supply chain syncing to prevent customer drop-off.

### 3. Recommended Actions

1.  **Prioritize Inventory Management Audit:** Immediately investigate the cause of frequent "out of stock" issues. A cross-functional review (Operations/Tech) is required to ensure real-time inventory synchronization between the kitchen and the customer-facing app interface.
2.  **Leverage Quality Proof Points:** Provide the Marketing team with the strong positive data and quotes regarding "freshness," "taste," and "packaging" to use as evidence in A/B testing copy and upcoming holiday campaign messaging.
3.  **Review Geo-Fencing and Expansion Strategy:** Analyze service gaps reported in App & Location Issues (e.g., Brookfield). Define a clear timeline for strategic expansion or implement clearer messaging in the app when an area is temporarily or permanently out of range.
