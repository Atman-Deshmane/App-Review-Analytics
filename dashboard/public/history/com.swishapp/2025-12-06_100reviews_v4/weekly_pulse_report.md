## Weekly App Review Pulse: com.swishapp Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 23 Nov 2025 to 05 Dec 2025

### Executive Summary

Customer feedback this period (36 total reviews) was overwhelmingly driven by **Food Taste & Quality**, which received significant positive praise, reinforcing it as a core product strength and differentiator for Swishapp. However, targeted attention is required for minor operational friction points, specifically regarding inventory management, as indicated by reports of items frequently being "out of stock."

### 3. Recommended Actions

Based on the analysis of customer sentiment and thematic impact scores, the following actions are recommended:

1.  **Prioritize Inventory Synchronization:** Immediately audit app-side inventory synchronization protocols, focusing on reducing "out of stock" incidents, particularly for high-demand items or during peak operational hours.
2.  **Leverage Quality Feedback in Marketing:** Partner with the Marketing team to explicitly integrate the themes of "Freshness, Taste, and Proper Packaging" into upcoming acquisition and retention campaigns, utilizing the high-impact customer quotes.
3.  **Address Specific Coverage Gaps:** Initiate a feasibility study for expanding delivery zone coverage to known underserved areas (e.g., Brookfield), leveraging this information for strategic market growth planning.
