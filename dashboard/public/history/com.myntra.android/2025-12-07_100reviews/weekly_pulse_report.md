## Weekly App Review Pulse: com.myntra.android Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 26 Nov 2025 to 06 Dec 2025

### Executive Summary

App reviews during this period are dominated by critical failures in the customer support system, evidenced by the "Bad Customer Service" theme registering a disproportionate Impact Score of 7904. Customers are reporting severe difficulty accessing self-serve options and connecting with human agents, leading to high-visibility frustration that is compounding post-purchase operational issues, particularly around returns and refunds. We must treat the broken support flow as a critical product failure requiring immediate mitigation.

### 3. Recommended Actions

Based on the immediate and critical impact of the support failures, the following actions are recommended:

1.  **URGENT: Repair the Contact Flow Functionality:** Immediately escalate and fix the specific product failure in the "Contact Us" pop-up flow to ensure customers can select a query type and initiate contact, especially for high-priority actions like connecting to a live agent.
2.  **Audit End-to-End Return Resolution Path:** Conduct a deep dive into the technical trigger behind the 'Return Picked Up Failed' status. Simultaneously, ensure that any canceled or failed return immediately auto-generates a high-priority, dedicated, and easily accessible support ticket pathway to prevent customers from being stranded without resolution options.
3.  **Investigate Recent UX/UI Updates:** Launch a targeted review of all changes introduced in the most recent app update, specifically focusing on simplifying navigation and reducing perceived complexity cited by the large volume of 'Other' reviews. Prioritize user pathways that were recently altered.
