## Weekly App Review Pulse: com.healthifyme.basic Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 02 Mar 2026 to 14 Mar 2026

### Executive Summary
This reporting period highlights continued user concern regarding pricing and value, with a significant negative impact from customer support issues. While feature requests and usability remain areas for attention, the decline in perceived value and customer service are the most critical areas demanding immediate focus.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights

*   **Customer Support (Impact Score: 3, Review Count: 4):** This theme emerges as the most critical this period due to a highly negative customer quote highlighting unaddressed refund issues and lack of proper response. The specific case of a cancelled order and unfulfilled refund signifies a significant breakdown in customer trust.
*   **Pricing & Value (Impact Score: 0, Review Count: 3):** A prominent quote from a long-term user expresses deep disappointment with the shift from free to paid features, indicating a potential alienating effect on loyal users who feel the core functionality is now inaccessible without payment.
*   **Feature Issues (Impact Score: 1, Review Count: 5):** While the impact score is lower, the review count is the highest among the top themes. A specific request for a Glycemic Index option suggests a valuable feature enhancement that could improve user understanding of food quality and contribute to better health outcomes.

### 2. Voice of the Customer (Quotes)

*   **Customer Support:** "Terrible customer service – refund not processed I cancelled my order #HSF115794 for the FreeStyle Libre Smart CGM Sensor and the support team confirmed the cancellation. However, the app still shows it as fulfilled and my refund of ₹4,099 has not been processed. I have sent multiple reminders but received no proper response. Very disappointing customer service. I would advise others to be cautious when ordering from HealthifyMe."
*   **Pricing & Value:** "I had been using the app for last 6-7 years. It used to be such a great one to track your calories and get insights on your macros and micros. Now all they want is money, everything that was free earlier is paid now. I just want to count my calories and watch my intake of macros and micros, why do I need to pay for it? I am not asking for any other service, no coaches, no meal plans nothing. So disappointed, had to uninstall after such long use. It was good while it lasted."
*   **Feature Issues:** "I am a user from 2017. Fitness improves both physical and mental strength. Studies show that fit people are often more successful than unfit ones. With the premium plan, you can become fit within a few months. Coaches call or message at your convenient time and keep motivating you toward your goals. I request the developers to add a Glycemic Index option for foods and meals to help users judge food quality better."

### 3. Recommended Actions

*   **Immediate Customer Support Audit:** Conduct a thorough review of the customer support process, focusing on order cancellations and refund fulfillment. Prioritize resolving the specific issue highlighted in the quote to prevent further negative sentiment and potential churn.
*   **Re-evaluate Pricing and Value Proposition:** Analyze user sentiment regarding the shift to paid features. Explore options for reintroducing some core calorie/macro tracking functionalities as free or at a more accessible price point for long-term users.
*   **Prioritize Glycemic Index Feature Integration:** Investigate the feasibility and potential impact of adding a Glycemic Index option for foods and meals. This feature addresses a specific user need and could enhance the app's value proposition for health-conscious users.
