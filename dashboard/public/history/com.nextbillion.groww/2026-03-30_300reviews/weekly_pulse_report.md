## Weekly App Review Pulse: Groww Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 16 Mar 2026 to 29 Mar 2026

### Executive Summary
App Glitches continue to dominate user sentiment, significantly impacting the trading experience, particularly during critical market events like new listings. While Feature Requests show moderate user interest, the severity of App Glitches overshadows positive feedback on the platform's user-friendliness. Addressing these critical performance issues is paramount for maintaining user trust and retention.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights

*   **App Glitches (Impact Score: 896, Reviews: 50):** This is by far the most critical theme impacting user experience. The overwhelmingly high impact score and associated review count highlight severe operational issues. The top quote vividly illustrates how even minor delays during new stock listings can lead to significant frustration and lost opportunities for active traders, directly impacting their ability to execute strategies and potentially their profitability. This theme demands immediate and focused attention to ensure app stability and responsiveness.

*   **Feature Requests (Impact Score: 134, Reviews: 25):** While the impact score is considerably lower than App Glitches, there's a consistent stream of user suggestions for enhancements. The top quote indicates a desire for more detailed stock information, such as all-time low/high ranges and clear categorization (large-cap, mid-cap, small-cap). This suggests an opportunity to improve the depth of information provided within the app, catering to users who want to make more informed investment decisions.

*   **Customer Support (Impact Score: 25, Reviews: 11):** Although the impact score and review count are relatively low, the top quote reveals a deeply concerning experience where delayed support led to financial loss for a user due to app hangs during mutual fund redemption. This points to potential issues with the efficiency and effectiveness of the customer support process, particularly when combined with app performance problems. Even a small number of such incidents can severely damage user confidence and brand reputation.

### 2. Voice of the Customer (Quotes)

*   "On the listing day, the stock was not visible on the Groww app for around 10 minutes after it went live on the exchange. During the same time, it was available for trading on other brokerage platforms. This delay prevented me from entering at my intended price. For active traders, even a few minutes matter. I hope necessary improvements are made to avoid such delays in future listings." (Impacts "App Glitches")

*   "I am very upset with customer support whose delay has resulted in financial loss to me. My Groww app hanged from 2.30pm onwards today while I was trying to redeem mutual funds. I tried relogging but it didn't help. I immediately called customer care but after 5-6 minutes the executive just stopped talking to me and transferred my call to IVR. I opened Customer Support chat at 2.46 pm but they could resolve my issue by 3.10pm by then I lost opportunity to get today's NAV for no fault of mine." (Impacts "Customer Support" and "App Glitches")

*   "All-time low to high range should be mentioned along with the day and 52-week price ranges. Also, the company category, such as large-cap, mid-cap, or small-cap, should be clearly specified along with the stock name for better clarity." (Impacts "Feature Requests")

### 3. Recommended Actions

*   **Prioritize and Expedite Fixes for Critical App Glitches:** Allocate immediate engineering resources to address the recurring "App Glitches," with a specific focus on improving performance during new stock listings and periods of high trading activity. Implement robust monitoring and alert systems to detect and resolve such issues proactively.
*   **Review and Optimize Customer Support Escalation and Resolution Processes:** Investigate the root causes behind the delayed resolution times and the reported unhelpful interactions with customer support, especially when app issues are involved. Streamline the escalation path and ensure support agents are equipped to handle critical situations effectively to prevent financial losses for users.
*   **Develop a Roadmap for Feature Enhancements Based on User Feedback:** While urgent bug fixes are paramount, begin planning for the integration of requested features like enhanced stock data (all-time ranges, market capitalization categories) to improve the platform's informational value and cater to more sophisticated user needs.
