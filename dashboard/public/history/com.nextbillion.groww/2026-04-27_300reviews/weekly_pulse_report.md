**Reporting Period:** 13 Apr 2026 to 26 Apr 2026

This reporting period highlights continued user satisfaction with Groww's "App Performance" and "Trading Features," with strong positive sentiment and high vote counts. However, "Customer Support" remains a critical area requiring immediate attention, as indicated by low impact and significant negative feedback.
