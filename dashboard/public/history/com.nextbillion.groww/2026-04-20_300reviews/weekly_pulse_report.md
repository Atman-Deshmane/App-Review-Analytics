## Weekly App Review Pulse: Groww Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 06 Apr 2026 to 19 Apr 2026

### Executive Summary
This bi-weekly review reveals a strong user appreciation for Groww's Trading Features, particularly highlighted by positive feedback on its comprehensive options trading capabilities. However, emerging concerns around App Performance, especially post-updates, and clarity on Charges and Fees require immediate attention to maintain user satisfaction.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights

*   **Trading Features (Impact Score: 146, Review Count: 20):** This remains our strongest performing area, with users actively praising the breadth and quality of our trading functionalities. The recent positive sentiment is directly linked to the platform's ability to support advanced trading strategies, as evidenced by the high votes on a quote specifically mentioning option basket features. This indicates a satisfied and engaged user base for our core trading product.

*   **User Interface (Impact Score: 105, Review Count: 56):** While the UI is generally well-received, a significant portion of reviews (56) indicate a need for refinement. The most voted quote suggests a clear demand for better organization, specifically the creation of a separate section for ETFs to improve user navigation and reduce clutter in existing views. This presents an opportunity to enhance user experience through targeted organizational improvements.

*   **App Performance (Impact Score: 47, Review Count: 27):** This theme, while lower in overall impact score, carries a critical recent negative sentiment. The top quote for App Performance, with 10 votes, describes significant issues including symbols not loading, slowdowns after updates, and UI disruptions. This indicates a potential degradation in user experience that could impact broader satisfaction and retention if not addressed promptly.

### 3. Recommended Actions

*   **Prioritize App Performance Fixes:** Immediately investigate and address the reported issues of slow loading times, symbols not appearing, and UI disruptions following recent updates. Implement a robust rollback or hotfix strategy if a specific update is identified as the root cause.
*   **Enhance UI Organization for ETFs:** Action the user feedback by designing and implementing a dedicated section for ETFs within the app to improve navigation and reduce clutter for users managing diverse portfolios.
*   **Review and Communicate Charges Clarity:** Proactively review the "Charges and Fees" theme for any recurring user confusion or dissatisfaction, and consider clearer in-app explanations or FAQs regarding brokerage and other charges to mitigate negative sentiment.
