## Weekly App Review Pulse: Groww Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 11 May 2026 to 24 May 2026

### Executive Summary
This reporting period highlights a significant concern around **App Performance Issues**, specifically the persistent failure of ALERT notifications, which is impacting user experience. While overall review volume remains high, the recurring nature of critical bugs like the notification issue demands immediate attention to maintain user trust and engagement.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights
*   **App Performance Issues (Impact Score: 397, Review Count: 31):** This theme emerges as the most critical concern, driven by a significant number of reviews, particularly the recurring issue with ALERT notifications not functioning despite being enabled. This directly impacts users' ability to act on market opportunities and is likely contributing to frustration and a perception of unreliability.

*   **Other (Impact Score: 227, Review Count: 207):** While the "Other" category has the highest review count, its lower impact score suggests a wider range of less critical, miscellaneous feedback. However, the top quote within this category, "Sometimes they are cheating our trading money," warrants further investigation as it points to a serious trust issue.

*   **Trading Feature Requests (Impact Score: 71, Review Count: 25):** Users, particularly those identifying as serious traders, are expressing dissatisfaction with the current charting capabilities, citing a lack of advanced indicators and overall quality compared to competitors. This suggests an opportunity to enhance the platform's appeal to a more sophisticated trading demographic.

### 3. Recommended Actions
*   **Prioritize and resolve the ALERT notification bug immediately.** Dedicate engineering resources to a comprehensive investigation and fix, ensuring thorough testing before deployment.
