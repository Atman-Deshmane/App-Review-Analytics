## Weekly App Review Pulse: Groww Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 15 Dec 2025 to 28 Dec 2025

### Executive Summary
App performance remains the dominant concern, with a significant number of users highlighting a lack of updates and basic feature limitations, particularly around charting and trading tools. While trading features are generally well-received, there's a clear demand for enhancements like advanced charting, improved alert management, and additional investment options. Customer support continues to be a pain point, with frequent complaints about unresponsiveness and abrupt chat closures.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights

*   **App Performance (Impact Score: 1014, Reviews: 109):** This theme continues to be the most critical area for improvement. The overwhelmingly negative sentiment stems from the perceived stagnation of the app, with users explicitly mentioning a lack of updates to core features like charts and a desire for advanced trading functionalities such as a trader's diary and multiple watchlists. The absence of essential features like chart alerts and notes on watchlists further compounds user frustration.
*   **Trading Features (Impact Score: 922, Reviews: 68):** While the core UX is appreciated, users are requesting specific feature additions that would significantly enhance their trading experience. The desire for features like LRS for foreign MF investments, better display of sector information, and more robust stock price alert management indicates a segment of users looking for Groww to cater to more sophisticated trading needs.
*   **Customer Support (Impact Score: 123, Reviews: 36):** Despite a lower review count than other themes, the impact score and the qualitative feedback reveal a significant issue. Users are experiencing extreme unresponsiveness and a perceived lack of genuine interest in resolving their issues. The abrupt closure of chats without confirmation of resolution is particularly damaging to user satisfaction and trust.

### 3. Recommended Actions

*   **Prioritize and accelerate the roadmap for app performance enhancements, specifically focusing on charting capabilities and the introduction of advanced trading features** such as a trader's diary, multiple watchlists, and improved alert management.
*   **Implement immediate improvements to customer support processes, focusing on increasing responsiveness, ensuring resolution confirmation, and enabling follow-up interactions.** Explore training and performance metrics for support executives related to customer satisfaction and issue resolution.
*   **Conduct user research to validate and prioritize the requested trading feature enhancements**, such as LRS for foreign MF, improved sector information display, and streamlined alert management, to inform future product development.
