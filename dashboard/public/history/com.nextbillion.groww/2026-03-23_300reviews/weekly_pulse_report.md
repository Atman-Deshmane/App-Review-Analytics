## Weekly App Review Pulse: Groww Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 09 Mar 2026 to 22 Mar 2026

### Executive Summary
This reporting period saw a significant surge in reviews related to "Other" themes, heavily influenced by overwhelmingly positive feedback on the app's core user experience and the Groww team's responsiveness. However, critical issues persist in "App Performance," with users reporting significant losses due to technical errors, and "Account Features," where a lack of functionality is impacting investment tracking.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights

*   **Other (Impact: 1786, Reviews: 192):** This theme dominated user feedback, driven by an exceptional sentiment. The top quote highlights a user's long-standing loyalty and satisfaction, praising the app's ease of use, responsiveness, and efficient resolution of issues. This indicates strong brand advocacy and a solid foundation for user engagement, but also underscores the potential impact if core issues in other areas are not addressed.

*   **App Performance (Impact: 372, Reviews: 43):** Despite a lower review count, this theme carries a substantial impact score, indicating critical user pain points. The top quote explicitly details technical errors that directly lead to financial losses for users, who are unable to exit markets in time. The inability to provide supporting evidence (screenshots) further frustrates users and highlights a potential gap in issue reporting or resolution.

*   **Account Features (Impact: 237, Reviews: 36):** This theme also presents a significant concern. Users are expressing frustration over the disabling of key features like exporting external funds and are seeking clarity on timelines for their re-enablement. Furthermore, the dashboard displaying outdated portfolio data hinders effective investment tracking, a core function for users.

### 2. Voice of the Customer (Quotes)

*   **Other:** "Growing from 2018, this is the BEST app. Very honest application, makes it very easy and fast. And after the working time, it also solves the problem very quickly. The charges are very high. Especially IETFs, unfortunately. But it is the best. Honest, helpful and fast team. And solves problems quickly. Chat facility is the best. Makes everything very easy." (Votes: 1763)

*   **App Performance:** "In this app, I often face technical errors when trying to exit the market, and because I’m unable to exit on time, I end up incurring losses. I also have screenshots of the technical error, but there is no option to attach them here. otherwise, I would have attached them." (Votes: 159)

*   **Account Features:** "The app was working just fine when they disabled the feature that would let you export the external funds as well. By when can we expect the feature enablement? The dashboard has old data for the portfolio that really doesn't help to track my investments." (Votes: 112)

### 3. Recommended Actions

*   **Prioritize and Resolve Critical App Performance Issues:** Immediately investigate and address the technical errors causing users to incur losses, particularly during market exit. Implement a mechanism for users to submit error logs or screenshots to aid in diagnosis.

*   **Expedite Account Feature Enhancements:** Provide a clear timeline for the re-enablement of the "export external funds" feature and communicate this to users. Simultaneously, address the outdated portfolio data on the dashboard to ensure accurate investment tracking.

*   **Leverage Positive Sentiment for Brand Advocacy:** While addressing critical issues, continue to foster the positive user experience highlighted in "Other" feedback. Consider proactive outreach to highly satisfied users for testimonials or feedback programs, reinforcing the app's strengths.
