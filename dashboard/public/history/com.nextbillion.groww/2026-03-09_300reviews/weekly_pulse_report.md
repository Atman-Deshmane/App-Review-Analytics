## Weekly App Review Pulse: Groww Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 23 Feb 2026 to 08 Mar 2026

### Executive Summary
This reporting period shows a significant user desire for new features, particularly **Basket Orders**, which garnered the highest impact score. While trading glitches remain a concern for active traders, **App Usability** continues to be a strong positive, highlighting Groww's accessibility for new investors.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights

*   **Feature Requests (Impact Score: 5501, Reviews: 23):** This theme clearly dominates user sentiment, driven by a highly impactful request for **Basket Orders**. The success of the recent addition of trailing stop loss indicates a strong correlation between fulfilling user-requested features and positive customer engagement.
*   **Trading Glitches (Impact Score: 1029, Reviews: 32):** Despite a lower review count than App Usability, the high impact score suggests that trading glitches, particularly delays in listing visibility, significantly frustrate active traders. This directly impacts their trading experience and potential P&L.
*   **App Usability (Impact Score: 516, Reviews: 52):** This remains a consistently strong area for Groww, with a high number of reviews indicating a positive user experience. The clean and straightforward interface is a key differentiator for attracting and retaining new investors.

### 2. Voice of the Customer (Quotes)

*   **Feature Requests:** "The most loved app by me. I want to request that you introduce basket orders as well. I am very happy that you all introduced trailing stop loss as per request. please introduce basket order as well" - This quote encapsulates the strong demand for new features and highlights the success of previously implemented requests.
*   **Trading Glitches:** "On the listing day, the stock was not visible on the Groww app for around 10 minutes after it went live on the exchange. During the same time, it was available for trading on other brokerage platforms. This delay prevented me from entering at my intended price. For active traders, even a few minutes matter. I hope necessary improvements are made to avoid such delays in future listings." - This quote vividly illustrates the critical impact of trading delays on active traders and their ability to capitalize on market opportunities.
*   **App Usability:** "I find the Groww app simple and easy to use. The interface is clean and straightforward, which makes it good for beginners who are just starting with investing or mutual funds. Navigating between stocks, mutual funds, and other investment options is smooth, and basic data like price charts and performance history loads quickly." - This quote reinforces the value proposition of Groww as an accessible and user-friendly platform, particularly for novice investors.

### 3. Recommended Actions

*   **Prioritize Development of Basket Orders:** Given the overwhelming impact score and user enthusiasm, expedite the development and release of Basket Orders to capitalize on this key feature request.
*   **Investigate and Resolve Listing Delays:** Conduct a thorough investigation into the root cause of the 10-minute listing delays and implement immediate technical solutions to ensure real-time availability of newly listed stocks.
*   **Continue to Leverage App Usability Strengths:** Maintain the focus on a clean and intuitive user interface, while exploring opportunities to further enhance the experience for both new and experienced investors based on ongoing feedback.
