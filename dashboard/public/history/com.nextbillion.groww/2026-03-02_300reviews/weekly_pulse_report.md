## Weekly App Review Pulse: Groww Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 16 Feb 2026 to 01 Mar 2026

### Executive Summary
App Performance Issues are a significant concern, with a high impact score, driven by user frustration over trading delays during listings. While the User Interface continues to be lauded for its ease of use, particularly for beginners, enhancements to Trading Features are actively sought, specifically regarding Options Chain data visualization.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights

*   **App Performance Issues (Impact Score: 1788, Reviews: 45):** This theme remains the most critical, indicating a clear need for immediate attention. The high impact score, primarily driven by specific user experiences during live trading events, suggests that even minor delays can lead to significant dissatisfaction and potential loss of trading opportunities for our active user base.
*   **User Interface (Impact Score: 776, Reviews: 198):** The consistent positive feedback on the UI highlights its strength in catering to new investors. The clarity and ease of analysis are key differentiators, however, it's important to monitor if this positive sentiment can be maintained as we introduce more advanced features.
*   **Trading Features (Impact Score: 748, Reviews: 25):** Although the review count is lower, the high impact score for Trading Features signals a strong desire for more sophisticated tools, especially in options trading. Users are specifically requesting enhanced data visualization for Open Interest (OI) on both charts and the option chain, indicating a gap in current offerings.

### 2. Voice of the Customer (Quotes)

*   **App Performance Issues:** "On the listing day, the stock was not visible on the Groww app for around 10 minutes after it went live on the exchange. During the same time, it was available for trading on other brokerage platforms. This delay prevented me from entering at my intended price. For active traders, even a few minutes matter. I hope necessary improvements are made to avoid such delays in future listings."
*   **User Interface:** "Groww is very handy and very clear app to avoid confusing even for the beginners to the market. Charts and other datas about a stock easy to analyse. I really love using it. Instant money withdrawal makes you feel better than other apps . I started my journey of stock market investment with Groww it will be very helpful for people like us who are not having any prior experience with market."
*   **Trading Features:** "i want to see oi on both chart of option price and index. this option chain is too big so update old option chain"

### 3. Recommended Actions

*   **Prioritize and Investigate App Performance for Live Trading Events:** Conduct a deep-dive into the root cause of trading delays during stock listings and implement robust technical solutions to ensure real-time availability across all trading scenarios.
*   **Enhance Options Trading Data Visualization:** Expedite the development and release of improved option chain functionality, including the requested OI data integration on charts and a more digestible option chain interface.
*   **Leverage UI Strengths for Upselling Advanced Features:** Explore ways to introduce more advanced trading features and data analytics without compromising the user-friendly experience that new investors currently value.
