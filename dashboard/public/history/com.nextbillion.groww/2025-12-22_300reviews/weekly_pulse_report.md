## Weekly App Review Pulse: Groww Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 08 Dec 2025 to 21 Dec 2025

### Executive Summary
This reporting period highlights an overwhelming user demand for enhanced **Trading Features**, particularly the addition of the stochastic indicator, which has garnered significant user attention. While overall **App Performance & Bugs** are a concern, a specific request for landscape mode support on tablets stands out. **Charges & Fees** transparency remains an area needing improvement for user understanding.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights

*   **Trading Features (Impact Score: 10617, Review Count: 49):** This theme dominates user feedback, indicating a strong desire for advanced technical analysis tools. The overwhelming impact score suggests that enhancing trading capabilities, such as adding requested indicators, could significantly boost user satisfaction and engagement.
*   **App Performance & Bugs (Impact Score: 350, Review Count: 42):** While the impact score is lower than trading features, the specific feedback on landscape mode for tablets points to a significant usability friction for a segment of users. Addressing this could improve the experience for tablet users and potentially reduce the need to use the web version.
*   **Charges & Fees (Impact Score: 280, Review Count: 19):** The consistent feedback on the lack of transparency in charges and fees suggests a need for clearer communication. Users express confusion regarding deductions, which can negatively impact their trust and overall app experience, despite liking other aspects of Groww.

### 2. Voice of the Customer (Quotes)

*   **"please add stochastic indicator, which is very useful, when you are going to add stochastic indicator?? Please update"** (5728 votes) - This quote directly reflects the overwhelming user desire for advanced trading indicators, demonstrating a strong demand within the user base.
*   **"To Team Groww, I request you to kindly update the UI of the app , allowing us to use the app in landscape mode as well. This will be helpful in charting and easier to use in tablet. Currently the app only works in vertical portrait mode which is really troublesome to use in my Tablet and i have to login via web to operate properly."** (190 votes) - This quote highlights a clear usability issue for tablet users, indicating a segment that is experiencing significant friction and resorting to alternative methods to use the app effectively.
*   **"I like using the Groww app, but I have one major issue. The charges that get deducted are not clearly explained. When any amount is charged, there are no proper details or breakdown provided. It becomes confusing to understand why the charges were applied. Please improve the transparency of charges and provide clear details so users can track everything easily. Otherwise, the app experience is good, but this issue needs to be fixed."** (193 votes) - This quote encapsulates the core concern regarding charges and fees: a lack of clarity and transparency that leads to user confusion and dissatisfaction, even in an otherwise positive experience.

### 3. Recommended Actions

*   **Prioritize the development and integration of the stochastic indicator and explore other highly requested technical analysis tools** to address the dominant user demand in trading features.
*   **Investigate and implement landscape mode support for tablet users**, focusing on improving the charting experience and overall usability for this device segment.
*   **Enhance the transparency of charges and fees within the app**, by providing clear breakdowns and detailed explanations for all deductions to improve user understanding and trust.
