## Weekly App Review Pulse: Groww Leadership Briefing

**Reporting Period:** 22 Dec 2025 to 04 Jan 2026

### Executive Summary

This period sees a significant surge in negative sentiment, particularly around "Trading Features" and "App Performance Issues," driven by user frustration with charting capabilities and overall app glitches. While "Other" continues to generate positive reviews, the escalating concerns in core functionality threaten user retention.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights

*   **Trading Features (Impact Score: 1032, Review Count: 23):** This remains the dominant theme, with users expressing strong dissatisfaction regarding the lack of updates and essential trading tools such as a trader's diary, multiple watchlists, and effective alert options. The sentiment indicates a growing gap between user expectations for a robust trading platform and Groww's current offerings.
*   **App Performance Issues (Impact Score: 277, Review Count: 25):** The impact score for this theme has escalated, directly correlating with user complaints about frequent glitches, inability to view holdings, and poor charting performance (zoom functionality). This is a critical area impacting user experience and potentially driving churn, as evidenced by a user's threat to switch apps.
*   **Customer Support (Impact Score: 97, Review Count: 10):** While the review count is lower, the impact score combined with user feedback highlights significant issues with responsiveness and the perceived haste to close support chats. Users feel unheard and unable to resolve issues effectively, leading to frustration and a negative perception of Groww's service.

### 3. Recommended Actions

*   **Prioritize Trading Feature Enhancements:** Immediately address the most cited missing features in "Trading Features," such as a trader's diary, improved watchlist functionality, and alert options. Communicate a clear roadmap for upcoming feature releases to manage user expectations.
*   **Urgent App Performance & Charting Overhaul:** Dedicate engineering resources to resolve persistent glitches impacting holding visibility and improve charting performance, including zoom functionality. Conduct thorough quality assurance testing before deploying updates.
*   **Revamp Customer Support Protocols:** Implement mandatory training for customer support executives focusing on active listening, issue resolution, and post-chat follow-up. Establish clear guidelines to prevent premature chat closures and ensure users feel supported.
