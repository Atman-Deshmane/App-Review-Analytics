## Weekly App Review Pulse: Groww Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 04 May 2026 to 17 May 2026

### Executive Summary
This reporting period saw "Missing Features" emerge as the dominant concern, driven by user demand for enhanced stock profile information. While app performance remains a critical area, its impact score has decreased, suggesting some stabilization. Overall user sentiment is mixed, with "Other" categories contributing the highest review volume but with lower individual impact.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights

*   **Missing Features (Impact Score: 543, Reviews: 32):** This theme has become the most significant driver of negative user sentiment. Users are actively requesting more comprehensive information within stock profile pages, specifically about sectors, company fundamentals, and competitive landscapes. The high impact score indicates that addressing these feature gaps could significantly improve user satisfaction and engagement.

*   **App Performance (Impact Score: 144, Reviews: 40):** While App Performance remains a key concern, its impact score has reduced compared to previous periods. However, the "top quote" highlights a critical issue for scalpers, where performance degradation during profitable trading moments can lead to significant user frustration and potential financial losses. This indicates a need for continued focus, particularly on real-time stability during high-activity trading.

*   **Other (Impact Score: 96, Reviews: 200):** This theme garners the highest number of reviews, indicating a broad range of miscellaneous feedback. While the individual impact score is lower, the sheer volume suggests that addressing recurring issues within this category could still contribute positively to overall user experience. The provided quote points to a potential data inconsistency or a bug in account management for mutual funds.

### 3. Recommended Actions

*   **Prioritize the development of enhanced stock profile features:** Investigate and implement user-requested features such as sector identification, company fundamental summaries, and competitive analysis tools to directly address the leading "Missing Features" theme.
*   **Conduct a performance audit for high-frequency trading scenarios:** Focus on identifying and resolving performance bottlenecks, particularly buffering and app unresponsiveness, during peak trading times, with a specific emphasis on the experience of scalpers.
*   **Investigate and resolve reported data inconsistencies in account management:** Address the issue of mutual funds not updating correctly with new bank account details, and review the broader "Other" feedback for recurring, high-volume issues that can be rectified to improve the overall user experience.
