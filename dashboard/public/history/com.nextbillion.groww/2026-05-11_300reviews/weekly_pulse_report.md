## Weekly App Review Pulse: Groww Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 27 Apr 2026 to 10 May 2026

### Executive Summary
This reporting period highlights continued strong user engagement with a dominant "Other" theme. However, critical feedback in "Trading Issues" and "App Performance" points to significant friction points for active traders, requiring immediate attention to mitigate potential user churn.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights

*   **Other (Impact Score: 548, Reviews: 187):** This theme continues to be the most prevalent, indicating a generally positive user experience. The high impact score and review count suggest users are actively engaging with the app and finding value in its core functionalities. The top quote within this theme (468 votes) specifically requests enhanced stock profile information, including sector details and company fundamentals, showcasing a desire for deeper insights within the app.

*   **App Performance (Impact Score: 74, Reviews: 41):** While not the highest in volume, "App Performance" has a notable impact score, driven by user dissatisfaction with charting capabilities, particularly for intraday trading. The top quote (16 votes) explicitly states the application is "FULLY NOT FOR INTRADAY TRADING" due to limited and low-quality charting indicators compared to competitors. This is a significant concern for a core user segment.

*   **Trading Issues (Impact Score: 90, Reviews: 28):** This theme, despite a lower review count, carries a substantial impact score. The top quote (37 votes) details a severe issue with MCX contract prices getting stuck and failing to execute, leading to significant financial losses for the user. The frustration is amplified by unhelpful chat support and a threat to escalate to SEBI and MCX. This points to critical reliability issues in trading execution for certain instruments.

### 3. Recommended Actions

*   **Prioritize immediate investigation and resolution of "Trading Issues," specifically the MCX contract price halting and execution failures.** This requires cross-functional collaboration with the trading platform and technology teams to prevent recurring financial losses for users and potential regulatory scrutiny.
*   **Enhance charting functionalities for active traders by increasing the number and quality of technical indicators available.** Benchmark against leading competitor platforms to meet user expectations for intraday trading.
*   **Develop and implement the requested feature to display stock sector information and consider expanding to include fundamental company data (business description, moat, competition) within the stock profile.** This aligns with user requests from the dominant "Other" theme and can further differentiate Groww.
