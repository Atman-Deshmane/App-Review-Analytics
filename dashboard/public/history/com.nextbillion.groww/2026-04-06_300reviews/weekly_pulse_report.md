## Weekly App Review Pulse: Groww Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 23 Mar 2026 to 05 Apr 2026

### Executive Summary
This reporting period highlights a significant surge in user concerns around "App Performance Glitches," with an overwhelming impact score. While "Other" remains a broad category, the critical nature of performance issues demands immediate attention to maintain user trust and trading confidence.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights

*   **App Performance Glitches (Impact Score: 871, Reviews: 28):** This theme has emerged as the most critical issue. The provided quote details a specific instance of a stock being unavailable on Groww during a crucial listing period, directly impacting a user's trading opportunity. This indicates a potential vulnerability in our real-time data feeds or app responsiveness during high-volume events, which can severely erode confidence among active traders.

*   **Other (Impact Score: 852, Reviews: 231):** While this category is broad, the high impact score suggests underlying issues that may be related to the performance glitches or other unaddressed user frustrations. The top quote within "Other" points to ongoing concerns about brokerage, customer/product support, feature updates, and data accuracy.

*   **Trading Features (Impact Score: 69, Reviews: 19):** Although lower in overall impact and review count, the user feedback here is constructive and forward-looking. The request for specific indicators like "Bull bear power indicator" and "break down screener" indicates a segment of our user base actively seeking advanced tools to enhance their trading strategies.

### 3. Recommended Actions

*   **Prioritize immediate investigation and resolution of "App Performance Glitches,"** focusing on real-time data synchronization, app responsiveness during high-volume events, and latency issues, particularly for new listings.
*   **Conduct a deep-dive analysis into the "Other" theme** to identify common threads and address persistent user frustrations related to brokerage, support, feature velocity, and data accuracy.
*   **Evaluate the feasibility of integrating advanced "Trading Features"** such as the requested indicators and screeners to cater to the needs of our more sophisticated user base.
