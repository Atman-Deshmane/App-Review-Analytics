This review period highlights a significant surge in "Other" theme reviews, driven by a highly voted complaint about brokerage and support, overshadowing other areas. While User Interface remains a frequent topic, its impact score is considerably lower. App Performance also saw a notable increase in negative sentiment, indicating potential user frustration with stability and functionality.
