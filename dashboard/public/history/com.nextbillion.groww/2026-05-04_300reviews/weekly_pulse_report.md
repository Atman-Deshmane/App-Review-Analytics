## Weekly App Review Pulse: Groww Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 20 Apr 2026 to 03 May 2026

### Executive Summary
This reporting period, App Performance remains the dominant theme, driven by strong positive sentiment around ease of use and reliability. However, critical feedback on Trading Features and Customer Support highlights areas requiring immediate attention to enhance user experience and trust.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights

*   **App Performance (Impact Score: 349, Reviews: 105):** This theme continues to be a significant driver of user satisfaction, with the top quote praising the "easy to use and understand UI DESIGN, FAST & RELIABLE" nature of the app. This suggests that our core app experience is resonating well with users, contributing positively to overall sentiment.

*   **Trading Features (Impact Score: 117, Reviews: 32):** While fewer reviews focus on this theme, the impact is notable. Users are expressing a clear need for enhanced visibility within the holdings section, specifically requesting features like Zerodha Kite's display of total shares, invested amount, and P&L percentages on a single screen. This points to a desire for more sophisticated, at-a-glance data to inform trading decisions.

*   **Customer Support (Impact Score: 41, Reviews: 40):** Despite a lower impact score compared to performance, the direct and negative feedback on Customer Support is concerning. The quote highlights a perceived lack of knowledge and basic understanding from the support team, directly impacting user trust and the overall perception of Groww's reliability, especially in a critical year like 2026.

### 2. Voice of the Customer (Quotes)

*   **"easy to use and understand UI DESIGN, FAST & RELIABLE"** (App Performance, 142 votes) - This quote encapsulates the strong positive sentiment around our core app functionality and user interface, reinforcing our strengths.

*   **"In the holdings section, total shares, invested amount, total P&L in percentage, and day’s P&L in percentage should be shown, just like in Zerodha Kite. This essential information should be visible on a single screen, not after tapping or using filters. This is necessary to take appropriate action to exit from positions. Without this data, trading in Groww is not convenient."** (Trading Features, 63 votes) - This quote clearly articulates a specific, actionable gap in our trading features, directly impacting user convenience and decision-making.

*   **"Customer support is very pathetic even in 2026. So I'll be honest and give 1 rating for the weak customer help which matters the most. The team lacks a lot of knowledge and they don't know even the basics of MF and Stocks."** (Customer Support, 11 votes) - This quote is a strong indicator of a critical issue within our customer support operations, suggesting a significant gap in training and expertise that is actively harming our reputation.

### 3. Recommended Actions

*   **Enhance Trading Feature Visibility:** Prioritize the development and implementation of the requested display enhancements in the holdings section (total shares, P&L percentages) to match competitor benchmarks and improve trading convenience.
*   **Invest in Customer Support Training:** Conduct an urgent review and overhaul of customer support training programs, focusing on product knowledge (MF and Stocks basics) and problem-solving skills to address the persistent negative feedback.
*   **Leverage App Performance Strengths:** Continue to build upon the positive user experience of our UI and performance. Explore ways to highlight these strengths in marketing and communication to attract and retain users.
