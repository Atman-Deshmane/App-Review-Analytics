## Weekly App Review Pulse: Groww Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 18 May 2026 to 31 May 2026

### Executive Summary
This reporting period highlights significant user frustration with **App Performance Issues**, particularly concerning recent UI changes impacting trading flow and app stability. While **Trading Features** show positive engagement with feature requests, the overwhelming volume of reviews in the **Other** category indicates a potential need to address broader user sentiment or feedback loops.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights

*   **App Performance Issues (Impact Score: 74, Review Count: 31):** This theme is a critical concern, with users reporting instability, disruptive layout changes, and glitchy behavior specifically within the F&O section. The sentiment suggests that these issues are directly hindering the core trading experience for a substantial number of users. The lack of effective resolution from customer care further amplifies user dissatisfaction.

*   **Trading Features (Impact Score: 106, Review Count: 23):** Despite the performance issues, users are actively engaging with and requesting enhancements to trading features. The positive feedback and specific requests for advanced screeners (e.g., Break down screener, F&O sentiment screeners) indicate a desire for more sophisticated tools to aid trading decisions.

*   **Other (Impact Score: 187, Review Count: 222):** The highest review count and impact score in this category suggest a broad range of user feedback that falls outside specific functional areas. While the provided quote is about a previous version of Financial Performance, this high volume warrants further investigation to understand the underlying sentiment and identify any emerging trends or systemic issues impacting user satisfaction.
