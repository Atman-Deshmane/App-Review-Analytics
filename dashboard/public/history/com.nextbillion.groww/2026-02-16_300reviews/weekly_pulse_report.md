## Weekly App Review Pulse: Groww Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 02 Feb 2026 to 15 Feb 2026

### Executive Summary
This reporting period highlights a strong user focus on **User Experience** and **App Performance & Bugs**, with the latter seeing a significant increase in discussion. While overall sentiment remains positive, specific friction points in navigation and notification delivery are impacting user satisfaction. **Charges & Fees**, despite fewer reviews, continues to be a highly impactful theme, indicating strong user sentiment around value.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights

*   **User Experience (Impact Score: 2216, Review Count: 219):** This remains the dominant theme, with users expressing significant dissatisfaction regarding the app's usability. Key pain points include the inability to adjust font size, complex navigation to detailed charts, and the absence of saved chart settings. The lack of response to feedback further exacerbates this issue.
*   **App Performance & Bugs (Impact Score: 1586, Review Count: 51):** This theme has seen a notable rise in discussion and user feedback. Users report noticeable lag, delayed notifications (especially for price alerts and stock performance), and issues with alarm functionality where prices are skipped. The introduction of the technical section is a positive note, but performance issues are overshadowing it.
*   **Charges & Fees (Impact Score: 2071, Review Count: 18):** Although the review count is low, the impact score is exceptionally high, indicating that users who comment on charges and fees have very strong opinions. The feedback suggests a perception of high charges, despite the app's overall perceived value and ease of use, highlighting a potential disconnect between user perception and actual fee structures.
