## Weekly App Review Pulse: Groww Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 08 Jun 2026 to 21 Jun 2026

### Executive Summary
This reporting period highlights a significant user demand for enhanced **Trading Features**, particularly the inclusion of the Stochastic indicator, which has garnered an exceptionally high impact score. While User Experience remains a core strength, specific improvements in search functionality are requested. App Performance issues, though fewer in count, are generating notable user frustration.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights

*   **Trading Features (Impact Score: 5711, Review Count: 31):** This theme is overwhelmingly dominant this period, driven by a highly specific and vocal request for the Stochastic indicator. The massive impact score indicates that addressing this feature request could significantly improve user satisfaction and potentially attract new users.
*   **User Experience (Impact Score: 390, Review Count: 203):** While the app is praised for its overall ease of use, there's a clear opportunity to refine the search functionality for Mutual Funds. Users find it restrictive and less effective compared to competitors, suggesting a need for algorithmic adjustments to broaden search results.
*   **App Performance (Impact Score: 31, Review Count: 17):** Although the review count is relatively low, the recurring mention of loading and refresh issues upon app opening indicates a potential friction point for users. Addressing these performance glitches is crucial for maintaining a smooth and reliable user experience.

### 2. Voice of the Customer (Quotes)

*   **Trading Features:** "please add stochastic indicator, which is very useful, when you are going to add stochastic indicator?? *   **App Performance:** "is it it's a bug or it's normal every time I open app first it's loading then i have to refresh it in full network" - This quote directly addresses a recurring performance issue that impacts the initial user interaction with the app.

### 3. Recommended Actions

*   **Prioritize the integration of the Stochastic indicator** into the charting tools, as this is a clear and vocal user demand with a significant impact score.
*   **Investigate and enhance the Mutual Fund search algorithm** to provide more comprehensive and flexible results, benchmarked against competitor offerings.
*   **Address the reported app loading and refresh issues** to ensure a consistently smooth and immediate user experience upon app launch.
