## Weekly App Review Pulse: Groww Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 10 Feb 2026 to 22 Feb 2026

### Executive Summary
This reporting period saw a significant surge in user engagement with "Features & Tools," indicating a strong interest in enhancing analytical capabilities. However, concerns around "App Performance," particularly notification delays and alert accuracy, remain a key area for immediate attention. While overall sentiment for the app is positive, addressing these technical friction points is crucial for sustained user satisfaction.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights

*   **Features & Tools (Impact Score: 1970, Review Count: 67):** This theme continues to be a primary driver of user engagement and positive feedback. Users are actively seeking more advanced tools for technical and fundamental analysis. The recent addition of a technical section has been well-received, but the demand for shorter and longer timeframes, along with specific indicators like Open Interest and Fibonacci levels, highlights a clear opportunity for further feature development. Deepening fundamental analysis with insights like SWOT and forecasts could further solidify Groww's value proposition.

*   **Other (Impact Score: 1789, Review Count: 169):** This category, despite its broad nature, is heavily influenced by overwhelmingly positive sentiment and long-term user loyalty. The top quote reflects deep user satisfaction and appreciation for Groww's ease of use, reliability, and proactive problem-solving, even mentioning "charges are excessive" in a more neutral context within the broader positive sentiment. This indicates a strong foundation of user trust and satisfaction that can be leveraged.

*   **App Performance (Impact Score: 1315, Review Count: 35):** While the number of reviews is lower, the impact score and the detailed feedback from the top quote underscore the critical nature of performance issues. Notification delays, especially for price-sensitive alerts, are a significant pain point. The inaccuracy of price alerts (skipping over the target price) directly impacts user trading decisions and confidence. Improvements in this area are vital for maintaining the positive user experience driven by features and overall satisfaction.
