## Weekly App Review Pulse: Groww Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 01 Jun 2026 to 14 Jun 2026

### Executive Summary
This reporting period highlights a significant user demand for enhanced trading indicators, with "Trading Features" dominating the impact score. While app performance and UI remain areas for attention, the intensity of feedback around trading tools suggests a critical opportunity for product differentiation and user engagement.

### 1. Top Themes & Strategic Insights

*   **Trading Features (Impact Score: 5733, Review Count: 32):** This theme is the undisputed leader in user sentiment impact, driven by a strong desire for specific technical indicators like the Stochastic indicator. The high impact score indicates that advanced trading tools are a key driver of user satisfaction and potentially a significant factor in user retention and acquisition.
*   **App Performance (Impact Score: 85, Review Count: 39):** While not as impactful as trading features, app performance issues, including login problems and unexpected app closures, are frequently mentioned. These recurring issues can lead to user frustration and a perception of unreliability, impacting overall user experience.
*   **User Interface (Impact Score: 60, Review Count: 29):** User feedback suggests a decline in UI usability following recent redesigns. Specific complaints point to the removal of simultaneous point and percentage tracking for market movements and a more convoluted financial analysis view. This indicates a disconnect between design changes and user needs for clarity and ease of access.

### 3. Recommended Actions

*   **Prioritize and Fast-Track Trading Indicator Development:** Allocate dedicated resources to integrate highly requested indicators like Stochastic, and establish a clear roadmap for future indicator additions based on user demand to capture the significant impact score.
