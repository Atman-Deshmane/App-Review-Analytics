## Weekly App Review Pulse: com.spotify.music Leadership Briefing
**Reporting Period:** 25 Nov 2025 to 30 Nov 2025

### Executive Summary
Customer feedback this week is overwhelmingly dominated by extreme dissatisfaction concerning the quality of the Free Tier experience. While review volume was low (8 total), the theme "Free Version Restrictions" generated an impact score of 3045, highlighting critical frustration around skip limits, unexpected ad placement, and the paywall for downloads. Mitigation of excessive ad frequency and flow interruption is necessary to prevent the Free Tier from becoming an active deterrent rather than a conversion pipeline.

### 3. Recommended Actions

1.  **Re-evaluate Free Tier Gating:** Conduct a focused review of the free skip limitation (6 skips) and the inability to download any content. Determine if a minor concession (e.g., increasing skips to 10 or allowing limited single-playlist downloads) could significantly reduce customer frustration without undermining the Premium conversion value.
2.  **Audit Ad Flow Logic:** Investigate and correct ad insertion logic to eliminate mid-song interruptions and ensure all explicit promises of "uninterrupted listening" are strictly honored. Prioritize fixing the perception that Spotify is ad-heavy to protect the brand experience.
3.  **Reinforce Algorithmic Value:** Use the demonstrated strength of "Discovery" features as the primary selling point to justify Premium pricing, especially in light of competitive pressure regarding sound quality and new plan structures mentioned in other feedback ("New Plans & Pricing").
